My Story

I'm Miles,  and I am a fine- art inspired destination wedding and portrait photographer, based in London.I love to take pictures of happy people and be the part of their story, trying to convey a unique inner world of each person who I meet on my photo-journey. I shoot  both film and digital in light and tender colors  to document precious moments and tell my clients their story in the most beautiful and timeless way.Here you will find different series of photographs that reflect my style, and the way I see a wedding photography. I like to shoot small and cozy weddings, as well as large and noisy ones, in my country and overseas.

If our views coincide, and you feel that I am the photographer you were looking for, then send me an email and tell me about you and your wedding day. I like good stories. Let's talk, because this is where it all begins. If you have a personal query or would like to a personal quote, please fill out the form and I will get back to you as soon as possible.

Alternatively you can contact me directly on milesstone@weddingphotos.com
